Carrier Board Custom Expansion IF Connections

The connectors support the following.
> J20: CSI 1 x2 lane
> J21: CSI 1 x2 lane or 1 x4 lane
> Both J20/J21: C
- CAM_I2C, clock, and control GPIOs for the camera
- 3.3V Supply
